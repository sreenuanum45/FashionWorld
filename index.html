How to Measure

  1. Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your bust.
  2. Waist: Measure around your natural waistline.
  3. Hip: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  4. Shoulder: Measure from one shoulder edge to the other.
  5. Length: Measure from the base of your neck to your desired blouse length.

Note: All sarees come with standard 5.5 meters length. For custom lengths, please contact customer service.
